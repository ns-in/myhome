About / Description

Grahan is the base camp to Sar Pass and overall this place has trekkers from all around the globe! After reaching the village, one can enjoy the serenity of the lush greens and cannabis farming whilst indulging in raw honey tea & Maggi!

First day arrival is in Kasol, which is a picturesque village in Kullu, stretched along Parvati River and its 32 Kms from Bhunter (NH21). In recent years, Kasol has emerged as the favorite destination among young backpackers. Parvati valley is known for its deep green coniferous forests and turquoise blue water of Himalayan rivulets. An evening in Kasol is the best part of the trip, try out the cafes and Bakeries offering mouth watering Indian, Italian and Israeli food. Valley has Stay options for kind of budgets from Hotels, Resorts, Guest-houses, Hostels, camps and homestays.

About / Description

Kasol is a picturesque village in Kullu, stretched along Parvati River and its 32 Kms from Bhunter (NH21). In recent years, Kasol has emerged as the favorite destination among young backpackers. Parvati valley is known for its deep green coniferous forests and turquoise blue water of Himalayan rivulets. An evening in Kasol is the best part of the trip, try out the cafes and Bakeries offering mouth watering Indian, Italian and Israeli food. Valley has Stay options for kind of budgets from Hotels, Resorts, Guest-houses, Hostels, camps and homestays.

Kheerganga trek starts from the dam which is just ahead of Barshaini. At the dam you will see the confluence of Tosh Nalla and Parvati River. Turquoise water in the catchment area of the dam is a perfect place for a group picture. Now we descent to the bridge on Tosh Nalla and after the bridge we start our ascend to Nakthan village. From here almost flatish trail till we hit the meadows of Rudranaag. A small waterfall, meadows, few cafes and temple makes this an ideal place to break before we start our final ascend to Kheerganga. Soon after Rudranaag is a wooden bridge with Parvati flowing underneath in a narrow gorge. In Next few Kms we trek though the dense forest and gain elevation. There are many streams along the treks which flow from down the right and merging into Parvati flowing on your left. There are few dhabaas on the trek which serve snacks, water and juice. After few Kms of trek through the forest, the trek opens up into the meadows of Kheerganga, wide open space with multiple campsites and dhaabas, Mandir and hot water spring. The main attraction at Kheerganga is the pool of hot water fed by hot water springs. Just the thing you need when everything around you is chilling, Lie in the hot bath for a wonderfully soothing experience. There are separate pools for Girls and boys

Short Itinerary

Day 1 : Kasol >> Barshaini >> Nakthan >> Rudranaag >> Kheerganga (12 Km Trek)

Day 2 : Kheerganga >> Rudranaag >> Nakthan >> Barshaini >> Kasol (12 Km Trek)

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1 :
Breakfast in Kasol + Transfer to Barshaini village on Shared Cabs or local transport (20 Km).
Trek to Kheerganga is around 12 Kms, Most part of the trek is along side river Parvati.
Almost midway is a small meadow with a Rudranaag temple and a small waterfall.
Second half of the trek is little taxing, and goes through dense alpine forest .
On the top you are rewarded by the Open views of mountain ranges and hot springs .
Spend some time in the hot pool & Capture the Himalayan Silhouette during sunset.
There are couple of local dhabba's n Shops where you can grab hot Tea or a quick Snack.
Snacks + Dinner (Bonfire if possible / if Allowed).
Camping under the Stars at Kheerganga or Rudernaag.

Day 2 :
Sunrise Photography with panaromic view of the mighty Himalayas.
You may want to have another dip in the hot pool.
Breakfast at Kheerganga + Trek back to Barshaini Village (12km).
Taxis will be available at the dam for Transfer to Kasol (20 Km).
Collect Luggage + Departure for onward journey

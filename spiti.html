About / Description

Featuring fascinating valleys, green patches, quiet villages, and windswept landscapes, the mountainous chilly desert of Spiti is the synonym of incredible beauty. Influenced profoundly by Buddhism, this enchanting destination flaunts player flags hovering in the air and whitewashed Chortens featuring Buddhist relics.

Forming a major tourist destination of the country, Spiti Valley entices travellers with its serene Himalayan lakes, surreal moonscapes, beautiful monasteries, and exquisite passes. Draped in elegance and charm, Spiti welcomes a heavy influx of trekkers, pilgrims, and honeymooners from all over the world. Known for its rich culture, drool-worthy cuisine, and humble residents, Spiti Valley is gorgeous from every aspect a tourist can think of!

Nako Village: Highest village in the valley, Nako owns a lake formed out of the masses of the ice. Fauna including, Yaks, kine, horses and asses, can be found in abundance here.

Kibber: A beautiful village with the arid landscape, rocky mountains, meadows covered in vibrant wildflowers, and all this paired with the pristine pollution-free environment.

Tabo Monastery: Known as Ajanta of the Himalayas, Tabo Monastery is situated at a height of 3050 meters above the sea level. Located in the valley of Tabo, the monastery was founded back in 996 A.D.

Rampur: Rampur was once the capital of the princely State of Bhushar. It is one of the biggest commercial towns of Himachal Pradesh currently. You cannot miss visiting this place.

Package Summary

Adventure Himalayas offers you this amazing Spiti valley tour package from Chandigarh which will let you explore the 'Little Tibet' of India and give you once in a lifetime experience through the mountains and valleys of Himachal Pradesh.

During this trip, you will be visiting the ancient monasteries, which are famous all over the world like Tabo monastery. You will get to experience the traditional blend of Buddhism and Tibetian culture. You will be meeting the local people and will get to know about their lifestyle.you will also be witnessing the amazing wildlife of this place including the snow leopard.

During the trip, your comfort will be our priority you will be staying in a comfortable hotel/homestay and will get to eat delicious local food. Get ready to lose yourself amidst the beauty of this scenic valley. Send enquiry now and get a chance to create worth-sharing memories with your friends and family members.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1 - Arrival in Chandigarh
On the first day of your Spiti valley tour package from Chandigarh, on reaching the hotel in Chandigarh, meet our representatives and your fellow travellers. Enjoy the dinner and overnight stay.

Day 2 : Chandigarh to Narkanda.
On the second day of your Spiti valley tour package from Chandigarh, have your breakfast and kick start this adventurous tour. We would be reaching Narkanda by late afternoon. If the weather allows, we can visit the Hatu Peak. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 3 : Narkanda to Kalpa/Reckong Peo.
We will start riding towards the Sutlej River Valley after breakfast. It is a route that will mesmerise you with its beauty and will leave you amazed. Visit Suicide point and nearby areas later. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 4 : Kalpa to Tabo
This day starts with riding towards Hindustan-Tibet Highway. The way is famous for its mesmerising views and proximity to Tibet. Next, we will visit Nako, the last hamlet of Kinnaur district. We will also have a steaming hot lunch there. Night stay at a comfortable and authentic homestay.

Day 5 : Tabo to Kaza via Mudh
This day starts with a visit to the 1021 – year-old Tabo Monastery, UNESCO World Heritage Site. Tabo Monastery is located at the bottom of the valley and is a place which will help you learn more about Buddhism. Later, we will be visiting Pin Valley. Famous for its fauna, the valley is a great place to explore and find some of the endangered species of snow leopards. Hamlet of Mudh, the last motorable village of the Pin Valley awaits your presence as well.

Day 6 : Kaza(Kibber + Chicham + Langza + Hikkim + Komik)
This is going to be a long day and we will start early on this day. First, we will be visiting Hikkim, and send postcards from the World’s Highest Post Office. Next, we will visit the World’s Highest Village connected via motor-able road, Komik Adorable views of the Chau Chau Kang Nelda Park and The Buddha Statue is waiting for you at Langza. Overnight at a homestay in Kaza.

Day 7 : Kaza to Rampur/Sarahan
We will be driving towards Rampur, after breakfast. Rampur was once the capital of the princely State of Bhushar and is presently one of the biggest commercial towns of Himachal Pradesh. Located on the left bank of the river Sutlej, it is a great place to visit. This will be the longest route of the journey. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 8 : Rampur – Manali
On the eighth day of your Spiti valley tour package from Chandigarh, you will leave for Manali from Rampur. Manali takes about 7-8 hours to reach from Rampur. Dinner and overnight stay at Manali.

Day 9 : Manali – Delhi | Departure
Have your breakfast and use your leisure time to explore Manali. In the evening, we will leave for Delhi The tour ends with a lot of memories.
